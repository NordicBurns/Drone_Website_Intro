1. To Hover
- Place drone on flat surface
- Power drone ON
- Accelerate all motors to lift slightly off the ground
- Equalize speed of motors to hover
- Land
- Adjust any X, Y, or Z axis for any un wanted movements

2. To Fly
- Place Drone on flat surface
- Turn Drone ON
- Check all motors are functional
- Increase acceleration to lift off ground
- Increase throttle to move forward
- Decrease throttle to move backwards
- Land
- Make any adjustments needed to motors
- Adjust X, Y, and Z Axis if needed

3. To Land Safely
- Place Drone on flat surface
- Turn Drone ON
- Increase acceleration to lift off ground
- Decrease acceleration to decrease hieght
- Make sure drone lands safely
- Adjust Z axis to decraese or increase speed

4. To Detect Weather Forcast
- Program drone with a hygrometer aan anemometer
- Test both functions are working
- Take account for weather impact performance
- Fly Drone 400m high
- Record data for weather and any interferences
- Land
- Keep tweaking wherever needed
- Repeat
